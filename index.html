Digital Land

Digital Land is a team at the Ministry of Housing, Communities & Local Government working to improve land and housing data for the emerging proptech sector.

Our mission

The Digital Land team investigates ways to make land and housing data easier to find, use and trust so that:

  • proptech companies can create digital tools that make it easier to develop, sell, buy or rent
  • land/housing developers can comply with regulations and find opportunities to build
  • government can create policies and services that work for everyone

Users of land and housing data

Digital Land is developing data resources for 4 main groups of users:

  • Proptech developers

    Software developers who create digital tools that use land and housing data to make it easier to develop, buy, sell or rent property.

  • Data maintainers

    Developers complying with regulations, local authorities collecting land and housing data, and others submitting or updating data.

  • Data modellers

    People who create data specifications, such as those commissioned by councils to write the specifications for land and housing datasets.

  • Policy makers

    Government policy professionals who decide what and how data must be supplied and maintained by developers, local authorities and others in the housing sector.
